French Toast

Ingredients

  • Two slices of white bread
  • Two Eggs
  • Nutmeg
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Crack eggs into medium sized bowl
  2. add 3 teaspoons of Cinnamon
  3. add a tablespoon of Nutmeg
  4. add a teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  5. Cook on each side for 1-1 1/2 minutes
  6. Serve with syrup and/or butter
