Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Pancake Mix
  • Water

Directions

  1. Pour 1 cup of pancake mix into medium sized bowl
  2. Pour in about 2/3 cup of water into the medium sized bowl along with the pancake mix
  3. Stir until fully blended
  4. Pour pancake mix into frying pan on medium heat
  5. Cook on each side for 1-1 1/2 minutes
  6. Serve with syrup and/or butter
