Bacon and Eggs

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • Hickory smoked bacon
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Missing an Ingredient? Add here!



Directions

  1. Preheat one medium sized skillet on medium heat
  2. Crack eggs into medium sized bowl
  3. Add in teaspoon of salt and pepper, then beat egg mixture until fully blended
  4. Pour egg mixture into pan and flip eggs every few seconds until cooked to your prefence
  5. After removing eggs from pan, place bacon into pan
  6. Cook for two minutes on each side
  7. Enjoy!
